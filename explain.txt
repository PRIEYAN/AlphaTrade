 ALPHATRADE — WHAT IT IS & HOW TO USE IT  (plain English)
===============================================================================

WHAT IS ALPHATRADE?
-------------------
AlphaTrade is an AI helper for crypto trading on BNB Chain (it runs on the BNB
TESTNET by default — a practice network with free, fake coins, so you can try
everything with zero real money at risk).

Think of it as an assistant that:
  1. READS the market (how greedy/fearful people are, network conditions),
  2. THINKS using an AI (Groq) about what to do next,
  3. CHECKS that idea against YOUR safety rules,
  4. and only then SIGNS a trade with YOUR wallet — your keys stay with you.

The most important idea: the AI only SUGGESTS. It can never move your money on
its own. Every AI suggestion must pass your hard rules ("guardrails") first, and
nothing happens on-chain unless your own wallet signs it. You're always in
control, and there is a big "Kill Switch" to stop everything instantly.

WHAT IS IT FOR?
---------------
- Letting an AI watch the market and propose trades, while YOU keep the safety
  limits and the final say.
- Doing this self-custodially: no company holds your coins; your wallet signs.
- Competing in an on-chain trading competition on BNB Chain (you register your
  agent wallet, then your results are tracked).

THE TOP BAR (always visible)
----------------------------
- "Connect Wallet": links your crypto wallet (MetaMask / Trust Wallet). Nothing
  trades until you connect. On testnet, make sure your wallet is on "BSC Testnet"
  (the app shows a "Switch to BSC" button if it isn't) and grab free test coins
  from the faucet.
- "Kill Switch": one click stops the agent completely. While it's on, the AI
  refuses to produce any trade and nothing can be signed. Use it any time.

THE FOUR PAGES (left menu)
==========================

1) OVERVIEW  — "the dashboard / home screen"
   WHAT IT SHOWS:
     - Your real wallet balance (BNB) read live from the blockchain.
     - The live network status (which chain, current block, gas price).
     - The agent's state (Running / Paused) and your controls.
   HOW TO USE IT:
     - Connect your wallet to see your real balance.
     - Use Start / Stop / Autonomous / Kill Switch to control the agent.
     - Note: the portfolio history chart and profit (PnL) numbers are empty on
       purpose — they need saved history over time, and we never show fake
       numbers. They fill in only when real history exists.

2) GUARDRAILS  — "your safety rules"
   WHAT IT IS:
     - The hard limits the AI is NOT allowed to break. This is the safety net.
   THE RULES YOU SET:
     - Max % per trade        (e.g. never risk more than 10% in one trade)
     - Daily trade cap        (max number of trades per day)
     - Daily spend limit ($)  (max money spent per day)
     - Max drawdown %         (stop if losses get this deep)
     - Slippage %             (max price slippage you'll accept; capped at 5%)
     - Min confidence         (ignore the AI unless it's at least this sure)
     - Token allowlist        (only these coins are allowed)
   HOW TO USE IT:
     - Set your comfort levels and click "Save guardrails".
     - These rules are enforced by plain code (not the AI), so the AI can't talk
       its way past them. If a suggestion breaks any rule, it is REJECTED.

3) STRATEGY + AI  — "tell the AI your plan, get a decision"
   WHAT IT DOES:
     - You write your trading idea in plain English. The AI reads it plus the
       live market signals and proposes ONE action (buy / sell / hold).
   HOW TO USE IT:
     - Type your strategy, e.g. "Rotate up to 5% from USDT into BNB when Fear &
       Greed is above 60. Hold otherwise."
     - Pick which signals to use (Fear & Greed, Funding, Sentiment, Momentum).
     - Click "Run Analysis". You'll get one of:
         * GREEN "APPROVED"  = a real decision that passed your guardrails.
         * "REJECTED"        = the AI's idea broke a rule (it tells you which).
         * ORANGE "AI ERROR" = the AI couldn't run (usually a missing/invalid
                               GROQ_API_KEY) — nothing is faked.
     - If a trade is APPROVED, an "Execute via Trust Wallet" button appears so
       you can actually place it (this signs with your wallet).
   NOTE: With no AI key configured, this page honestly says AI is disabled — it
   will NOT invent a fake decision.

4) ACTIVITY  — "the proof / history page"
   WHAT IT SHOWS:
     - Competition: your registration status + a link to the on-chain contract.
       "Register Agent" puts your wallet into the competition.
     - Trade Log: your real recent on-chain transactions (from the explorer).
     - AI Decisions & x402 Payments: these are empty until that history is saved
       — again, no fake rows are ever shown.
   HOW TO USE IT:
     - Connect your wallet to see your real transaction history.
     - Click "Register Agent" to join the competition (needs the contract +,
       for a real on-chain register, Trust Wallet credentials; otherwise it
       clearly says "simulated").

WHAT YOU NEED FOR EACH FEATURE (optional keys)
----------------------------------------------
The app runs with NO keys (wallet connect + live balance + live network work).
Add keys to "switch on" more, each shows an honest "not configured" state until
you do:
  - Connect mobile wallet (QR) : VITE_WALLETCONNECT_PROJECT_ID  (cloud.reown.com)
  - Real AI decisions          : GROQ_API_KEY                   (console.groq.com)
  - Live Fear & Greed signal   : CMC_AGENT_API_KEY              (coinmarketcap.com)
  - Real trade history         : BSCSCAN_API_KEY                (bscscan.com)
  - Balances / trade signing   : TW_ACCESS_ID + TW_HMAC_SECRET  (trustwallet.com)
(See setup.txt for the full step-by-step, and info.txt for the technical detail.)

THE SAFETY PROMISE (in one line)
--------------------------------
AI suggests -> your guardrails (plain code) approve or reject -> your wallet
signs. Kill Switch stops it all. No fake data anywhere — if something isn't
available, the app says so instead of making numbers up. And by default it's all
on TESTNET, so you can practice with no real money.
